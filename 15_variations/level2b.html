THE EDENSPIEKERMANN MANIFESTO

       Edenspiekermann

         We don’t do good work.

          Good work is not enough; we need to do great work.

            We invent new tools.
             That may mean throwing out the old toolbox.


             We need

             inspiration to inspire.

             Share your experiences, ideas,

             failures, successes.We tolerate failure.

             Failure is

             part of the process.

             We collaborate.

             Collaboration does

             not mean
             consensus.We generate

             ideas.

             Idea generation

             is not idea selection.

             We like making stuff.

             Useful, beautiful,

             important things.

             We dare say no.

             Saying yes is often

             just laziness.

             We like surprises.

             We have to mistrust

             our own routines.

             It’s your company, too.

             If something can be done

             better, don’t wait

             for permission.
